Tandoori Chicken

A flavorful and smoky dish that is a favorite in Indian cuisine!

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp mustard oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

Prepare the Marinade:
  1. In a large bowl, mix yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric powder, garam masala, coriander powder, lemon juice, mustard oil, and salt.
  2. Add the chicken pieces to the marinade and coat them well.
  3. Cover the bowl and let the chicken marinate in the refrigerator for at least 6 hours or overnight for best results.
Cook the Chicken:
  1. Preheat your oven to 220°C (430°F).
  2. Place the marinated chicken pieces on a baking tray lined with foil.
  3. Bake the chicken for 25-30 minutes, turning once midway, until the chicken is cooked and slightly charred.
  4. For an authentic smoky flavor, place a piece of hot charcoal in a small bowl inside the tray, drizzle with a little ghee, and cover for 5 minutes.
Serve:
  • Garnish with sliced onions, lemon wedges, and fresh coriander leaves.
  • Serve hot with naan, roti, or rice.
Enjoy your delicious Tandoori Chicken!
