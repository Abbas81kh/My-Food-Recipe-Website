Jeera Rice

A fragrant and flavorful rice dish infused with cumin, perfect as a side for any curry!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 2 tablespoons ghee or oil
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 small bay leaf
  • 2 cups water
  • Salt to taste
  • Optional: fresh coriander for garnish

Instructions:

Prepare the Rice:
  1. Wash the basmati rice under running water until the water runs clear.
  2. Soak the rice in water for 20-30 minutes, then drain and set aside.
Cook the Jeera Rice:
  1. Heat ghee or oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add cumin seeds and sauté until they begin to splutter.
  3. Add the bay leaf and sauté for a few seconds.
  4. Add the soaked and drained rice. Gently stir for 1-2 minutes to coat the rice with the ghee and cumin flavor.
  5. Pour in 2 cups of water and add salt to taste. Mix well.
  6. Cover the pan and let the rice cook on low heat for 10-12 minutes or until the water is absorbed.
Serve:
  • Fluff the rice gently with a fork.
  • Garnish with fresh coriander if desired.
  • Serve hot as a side dish with your favorite curry or dal.
Enjoy your aromatic Jeera Rice!
