Making 'Roti'

A simple and traditional Indian flatbread recipe.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt (optional)
  • 3/4 cup water (adjust as needed)
  • 1 teaspoon oil or ghee (optional)
  • Extra flour for rolling

Instructions:

Prepare the Dough:
  1. In a large bowl, mix the wheat flour and salt.
  2. Gradually add water while kneading the mixture to form a soft, pliable dough.
  3. Add oil or ghee if desired and knead for a few more minutes until smooth.
  4. Cover the dough with a damp cloth and let it rest for 20-30 minutes.
Roll the Roti:
  1. Divide the dough into small, equal-sized balls.
  2. Lightly dust a rolling surface and rolling pin with flour.
  3. Roll each ball into a thin, round disc about 6-8 inches in diameter.
Cook the Roti:
  1. Heat a tawa or flat skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Place the rolled roti on the hot skillet and cook for 30 seconds.
  3. Flip the roti and cook the other side for another 30 seconds.
  4. Flip again and press lightly with a spatula; the roti should puff up.
  5. Remove from heat and keep warm in a covered container.
Serve:
  • Serve hot with curry, dal, or your favorite side dish.
  • Enjoy your homemade roti!
