Chicken Shawarma

A flavorful Middle Eastern dish made with marinated chicken, wrapped in flatbread with creamy sauces and fresh vegetables.

Ingredients:

For the Chicken:

  • 500g boneless chicken thighs
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Juice of 1 lemon

For the Wrap:

  • 4 pita bread or flatbreads
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup sliced cucumbers
  • 1 cup sliced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup sliced onions
  • 1/2 cup tahini sauce or garlic sauce

Instructions:

Marinate the Chicken:

  1. In a bowl, mix olive oil, paprika, cumin, turmeric, garlic powder, ground coriander, cayenne, salt, and lemon juice.
  2. Coat the chicken thighs with the marinade and let it rest for at least 2 hours (overnight for best results).

Cook the Chicken:

  1. Preheat a grill pan or skillet over medium heat.
  2. Cook the marinated chicken for 5-7 minutes on each side until fully cooked and slightly charred.
  3. Remove from heat and slice the chicken into strips.

Assemble the Shawarma Wrap:

  1. Warm the pita bread on a pan or in the oven.
  2. Spread a generous layer of tahini or garlic sauce over the bread.
  3. Layer with lettuce, cucumbers, tomatoes, onions, and the sliced chicken.
  4. Roll the wrap tightly and serve immediately.
Enjoy your delicious Chicken Shawarma with a side of hummus or fries!
