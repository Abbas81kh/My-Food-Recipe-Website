Palak Paneer

A healthy and flavorful Indian dish made with spinach and paneer.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups spinach (palak), blanched and pureed
  • 200 grams paneer, cubed
  • 2 tablespoons oil or ghee
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tomato, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 2 tablespoons fresh cream
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

Prepare the Spinach:
  1. Wash the spinach leaves thoroughly and blanch them in boiling water for 2-3 minutes.
  2. Drain and immediately transfer the spinach to cold water to retain the green color.
  3. Blend the spinach into a smooth puree and set aside.
Cook the Masala:
  1. Heat oil or ghee in a pan and add cumin seeds. Let them splutter.
  2. Add chopped onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  3. Stir in the ginger-garlic paste and cook for 1-2 minutes.
  4. Add tomatoes, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Cook until the oil separates from the masala.
Combine Spinach and Paneer:
  1. Add the spinach puree to the masala and mix well.
  2. Simmer the mixture for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Gently add the paneer cubes and garam masala. Cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  4. Stir in the fresh cream and turn off the heat.
Serve:
  • Garnish with a drizzle of fresh cream or butter.
  • Serve hot with naan, roti, or jeera rice.
Enjoy your delicious Palak Paneer!
