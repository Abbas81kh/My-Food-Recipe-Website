Gajar Ka Halwa

A classic Indian dessert made with carrots, milk, and love!

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg carrots, grated
  • 1 liter full-fat milk
  • 200 grams sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 100 grams ghee
  • 100 grams khoya (optional, for richness)
  • 50 grams chopped nuts (almonds, cashews, pistachios)
  • 1/2 tsp cardamom powder
  • A few strands of saffron (optional)

Instructions:

Cook the Carrots:
  1. Heat ghee in a large, heavy-bottomed pan.
  2. Add the grated carrots and saut√© on medium heat for 8-10 minutes until they soften slightly.
Add Milk and Simmer:
  1. Pour the milk into the pan and mix well with the carrots.
  2. Cook on low heat, stirring occasionally, until the milk reduces and thickens. This takes about 30-40 minutes.
Add Sugar and Flavorings:
  1. Stir in the sugar and cook for another 10-15 minutes, stirring frequently.
  2. Add the cardamom powder, saffron, and khoya (if using). Mix well.
Finish with Nuts:
  1. Garnish with chopped nuts and mix.
  2. Cook for another 5 minutes to enhance the flavors.
Serve:
  • Serve warm or chilled as a delightful dessert.
  • Enjoy your rich and flavorful Gajar Ka Halwa!
