Barfi

A rich and creamy Indian sweet made with milk solids and sugar, perfect for celebrations!

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups khoya (mawa)
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • 2 tablespoons ghee
  • Chopped nuts (pistachios, almonds) for garnishing
  • Silver vark (optional, for decoration)

Instructions:

Cook the Mixture:
  1. Heat ghee in a non-stick pan over medium heat.
  2. Add the khoya and saut√© for 3-4 minutes until it softens and releases a fragrant aroma.
  3. Add sugar and cardamom powder to the pan, and mix well.
  4. Continue cooking, stirring frequently, until the mixture thickens and leaves the sides of the pan.
Shape the Barfi:
  1. Grease a plate or tray with ghee.
  2. Pour the mixture into the tray and spread it evenly to your desired thickness.
  3. Garnish with chopped nuts and silver vark, pressing them gently into the mixture.
  4. Allow it to cool completely and set for about 1-2 hours.
Cut and Serve:
  1. Once set, cut the barfi into squares or diamonds using a sharp knife.
  2. Store in an airtight container or serve immediately as a delightful dessert.
Enjoy your creamy and delicious Barfi!
