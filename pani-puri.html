Pani Poori

A tangy and spicy street food delight loved across India!

Ingredients:

  • 1 pack of poori shells (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1 cup boiled and mashed potatoes
  • 1/2 cup boiled chickpeas
  • 1/2 cup tamarind chutney
  • 1/2 cup mint-coriander chutney
  • 1 tsp roasted cumin powder
  • 1 tsp chaat masala
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 4 cups cold water
  • 1/2 cup chopped coriander leaves

Instructions:

Prepare the Spiced Water:
  1. In a large bowl, mix mint-coriander chutney with 4 cups of cold water.
  2. Add roasted cumin powder, chaat masala, red chili powder, and salt.
  3. Mix well and chill in the refrigerator for 30 minutes.
Prepare the Filling:
  1. In a bowl, mix mashed potatoes, boiled chickpeas, and chopped coriander leaves.
  2. Add a pinch of salt and chaat masala for flavor.
Assemble the Pani Poori:
  1. Gently crack open the top of each poori shell to create a small hole.
  2. Stuff each poori with a small amount of the potato-chickpea mixture.
  3. Add a drizzle of tamarind chutney for sweetness.
  4. Dip the filled poori into the chilled spiced water or pour the water inside.
Serve:
  • Serve immediately to enjoy the crispy and tangy flavors.
  • Prepare a few at a time to prevent the poori from getting soggy.
Enjoy your homemade Pani Poori!
