Double Ka Meetha

A rich and delicious Hyderabadi dessert made with bread, milk, and dry fruits.

Ingredients:

  • 6 slices of bread, cut into halves
  • 2 cups full cream milk
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup ghee (clarified butter)
  • 1/4 cup condensed milk (optional for extra richness)
  • 1/4 cup chopped cashews
  • 1/4 cup chopped almonds
  • 1/4 cup raisins
  • 1/4 tsp cardamom powder
  • Saffron strands (optional)

Instructions:

Prepare the Bread:
  1. Trim the edges of the bread slices and cut them into halves or quarters.
  2. Heat ghee in a pan and fry the bread pieces until golden and crispy. Set aside.
Prepare the Milk Mixture:
  1. In a saucepan, heat milk and bring it to a boil.
  2. Add sugar, cardamom powder, and saffron strands (if using). Stir until the sugar dissolves.
  3. Simmer the milk for 5-7 minutes to slightly thicken it.
  4. Optional: Add condensed milk for extra richness.
Assemble the Dessert:
  1. In a serving dish, arrange the fried bread pieces in a single layer.
  2. Pour the warm milk mixture over the bread, ensuring all pieces are soaked.
  3. Sprinkle chopped cashews, almonds, and raisins on top.
Cook and Serve:
  1. Place the dish in a preheated oven at 180°C (350°F) for 10-15 minutes or cook on low heat on the stovetop for 5-7 minutes.
  2. Let the dessert cool slightly before serving.
  3. Serve warm or chilled as desired.
Enjoy your delicious Double Ka Meetha!
