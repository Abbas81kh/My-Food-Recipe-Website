Pasta - Macroni

A quick and delicious pasta recipe loved by all age groups!

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups macaroni pasta
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrot, bell pepper, corn)
  • 2 medium tomatoes, pureed
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 teaspoon red chili flakes
  • 1/2 teaspoon oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • Salt to taste
  • Grated cheese for garnish

Instructions:

Cook the Pasta:
  1. Bring a large pot of water to a boil and add a pinch of salt.
  2. Add the macaroni pasta and cook until al dente, as per the package instructions.
  3. Drain the pasta and set aside.
Prepare the Sauce:
  1. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add the chopped onion and garlic, and saut√© until golden brown.
  3. Add the mixed vegetables and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Stir in the tomato puree, tomato sauce, chili flakes, oregano, black pepper, and salt. Cook for 5 minutes.
Combine:
  1. Add the cooked macaroni pasta to the prepared sauce.
  2. Mix well, ensuring the pasta is evenly coated with the sauce.
  3. Cook for another 2 minutes and remove from heat.
Serve:
  • Garnish with grated cheese and serve hot.
  • Enjoy your delicious macaroni pasta!
