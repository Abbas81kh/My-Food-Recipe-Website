Fried Rice

A savory and aromatic dish packed with vegetables and seasonings, perfect for any meal!

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably day-old)
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1/2 cup diced carrots
  • 1/2 cup peas
  • 1/4 cup chopped green beans
  • 1/4 cup chopped spring onions
  • 2 teaspoons soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon chili sauce
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: scrambled eggs or diced chicken

Instructions:

Prepare the Vegetables:
  1. Heat oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add minced garlic and grated ginger. Saut√© for 30 seconds until aromatic.
  3. Add carrots, peas, and green beans. Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until slightly tender.
  4. Add spring onions and continue cooking for 1-2 minutes.
Cook the Fried Rice:
  1. Push the vegetables to one side of the pan. Add a little oil to the empty side.
  2. Optional: Add scrambled eggs or diced chicken and cook for 1-2 minutes.
  3. Add the cooked rice to the pan and mix everything well.
  4. Pour in the soy sauce and chili sauce. Toss to coat the rice evenly.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
Serve:
  • Garnish with additional spring onions or sesame seeds if desired.
  • Serve hot as a standalone dish or alongside your favorite main course.
Enjoy your delicious Fried Rice!
