Kheer

A creamy and delightful Indian dessert made with rice, milk, and sugar.

Ingredients:

  • 1 liter full-fat milk
  • 1/4 cup basmati rice
  • 1/2 cup sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 2-3 green cardamom pods, crushed
  • 10-12 almonds, chopped
  • 10-12 cashews, chopped
  • 10-15 raisins
  • A pinch of saffron strands (optional)
  • 2 tbsp ghee

Instructions:

Prepare the Ingredients:
  1. Rinse the basmati rice under cold water and soak it for 20-30 minutes.
  2. Chop the almonds and cashews, and set them aside.
Cook the Kheer:
  1. In a heavy-bottomed pan, heat the ghee and lightly fry the almonds, cashews, and raisins until golden. Remove and set aside.
  2. In the same pan, bring the milk to a boil, then lower the heat and let it simmer.
  3. Drain the soaked rice and add it to the simmering milk. Stir well to avoid lumps.
  4. Cook on low heat, stirring frequently, until the rice is soft and the milk thickens (about 25-30 minutes).
  5. Add sugar, crushed cardamom, and saffron strands. Mix well and cook for another 5-7 minutes.
  6. Finally, add the fried dry fruits and mix gently.
Serve:
  • Let the kheer cool slightly and serve warm or chilled.
  • Garnish with additional dry fruits or saffron strands before serving.
Enjoy your delicious homemade Kheer!
