Malai Kofta

A rich and creamy North Indian curry with deep-fried potato and paneer balls served in a delicious tomato-based gravy.

Ingredients:

Kofta:

  • 2 cups boiled and mashed potatoes
  • 1 cup grated paneer
  • 2 tablespoons cornflour
  • 2 tablespoons raisins (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for frying

Gravy:

  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 cup finely chopped onions
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 cups tomato puree
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 cup fresh cream
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

Kofta:

  1. In a bowl, mix potatoes, paneer, cornflour, salt, and raisins.
  2. Shape the mixture into small balls.
  3. Heat oil in a deep pan and fry the balls until golden brown. Set aside.

Gravy:

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds.
  2. Once they splutter, add onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 1 minute.
  4. Stir in tomato puree, turmeric powder, and garam masala. Cook for 5-7 minutes.
  5. Add cream and mix well. Simmer for 2 minutes.

Combine:

  1. Add the fried koftas to the gravy just before serving.
  2. Garnish with cream or coriander leaves.
Enjoy your delicious Malai Kofta with naan or rice!
