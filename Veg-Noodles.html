Veg Noodles

A quick and delicious stir-fry recipe loaded with fresh vegetables and Asian flavors!

Ingredients:

  • 200g noodles
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 cup sliced bell peppers (red, yellow, green)
  • 1/2 cup julienned carrots
  • 1/2 cup shredded cabbage
  • 1/4 cup sliced spring onions
  • 2 teaspoons soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon chili sauce
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

Cook the Noodles:
  1. Boil water in a large pot and cook the noodles according to the package instructions.
  2. Drain the noodles and rinse under cold water to prevent sticking.
  3. Toss with a few drops of oil and set aside.
Prepare the Stir-Fry:
  1. Heat oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add minced garlic and saut√© for 30 seconds until fragrant.
  3. Add the carrots, cabbage, and bell peppers, and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Add spring onions and continue cooking for 1-2 minutes.
Combine and Serve:
  1. Add the cooked noodles to the stir-fried vegetables.
  2. Pour in the soy sauce, vinegar, and chili sauce. Mix well.
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Stir-fry for another 2 minutes to combine all flavors.
Serve:
  • Garnish with additional spring onions or sesame seeds if desired.
  • Serve hot with your favorite side dish or enjoy as a standalone meal.
Enjoy your delicious Veg Noodles!
