Rajma Chawal

A comforting and hearty North Indian dish of red kidney beans served with rice!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup red kidney beans (rajma), soaked overnight
  • 3 cups water
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 medium tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Cooked rice, for serving
  • Fresh coriander leaves, for garnish

Instructions:

Prepare the Rajma:
  1. Drain the soaked rajma and rinse it well.
  2. In a pressure cooker, add the rajma and 3 cups of water. Pressure cook for 15-20 minutes until the beans are soft.
Make the Masala:
  1. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat and add cumin seeds. Let them splutter.
  2. Add chopped onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  3. Stir in the ginger-garlic paste and cook for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  4. Add the tomato puree, red chili powder, turmeric powder, coriander powder, and salt. Cook until the oil separates from the masala.
Combine and Cook:
  1. Add the cooked rajma along with its water to the masala. Mix well.
  2. Let it simmer on low heat for 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally. Adjust the consistency by adding more water if needed.
  3. Sprinkle garam masala and mix well.
Serve:
  • Serve hot with steamed rice.
  • Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
Enjoy your comforting Rajma Chawal!
