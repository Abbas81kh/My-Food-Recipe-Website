Shahi Paneer

A rich and creamy North Indian curry that is truly royal in flavor!

Ingredients:

  • 200g paneer (cottage cheese), cubed
  • 2 tbsp butter or ghee
  • 2 medium onions, finely chopped
  • 2 medium tomatoes, pureed
  • 1/4 cup cashew paste (soaked and blended cashews)
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp sugar
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions:

Prepare the Base:
  1. Heat butter or ghee in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add cumin seeds and let them crackle.
  3. Add the chopped onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  4. Mix in the ginger-garlic paste and cook until the raw smell disappears.
  5. Add the tomato puree and cook until the oil separates.
Prepare the Gravy:
  1. Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, garam masala, and salt. Stir well.
  2. Mix in the cashew paste and cook for 3-4 minutes.
  3. Add sugar and 1/2 cup water to adjust the consistency.
  4. Stir in the heavy cream and mix until the gravy is smooth and creamy.
Add Paneer and Serve:
  1. Gently add the paneer cubes to the gravy and simmer for 5 minutes.
  2. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  3. Serve hot with naan, roti, or steamed rice.
Enjoy your luxurious Shahi Paneer!
