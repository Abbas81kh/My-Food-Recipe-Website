Dhokla

A soft and fluffy steamed snack from Gujarat, perfect for breakfast or tea-time!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup gram flour (besan)
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 tsp ginger-green chili paste
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp fruit salt (Eno)
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

Prepare the Batter:
  1. In a mixing bowl, combine gram flour, yogurt, water, ginger-green chili paste, sugar, turmeric powder, and salt.
  2. Mix well to form a smooth batter without lumps. Let it rest for 10 minutes.
Steam the Dhokla:
  1. Grease a round steaming plate or tray with oil.
  2. Just before steaming, add fruit salt (Eno) to the batter and mix gently.
  3. Pour the batter into the greased plate and place it in a steamer.
  4. Steam for 12-15 minutes or until a toothpick inserted comes out clean.
  5. Remove and let it cool slightly before cutting into squares.
Prepare the Tempering:
  1. Heat 1 tbsp oil in a pan and add 1 tsp mustard seeds.
  2. When the seeds crackle, add 10-12 curry leaves and 2 slit green chilies. Saut√© for a few seconds.
  3. Pour the tempering over the steamed dhokla.
Serve:
  • Garnish with freshly chopped coriander leaves and grated coconut (optional).
  • Serve with green chutney or tamarind chutney.
Enjoy your soft and spongy Dhokla!
