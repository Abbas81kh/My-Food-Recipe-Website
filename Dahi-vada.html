Dahi Vada

A refreshing Indian snack made of lentil dumplings soaked in yogurt and topped with tangy chutneys!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup urad dal (split black gram), soaked for 4-6 hours
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • A pinch of asafoetida (hing)
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • Oil for deep frying
  • 2 cups yogurt (curd), whisked
  • 1/4 cup tamarind chutney
  • 1/4 cup green chutney
  • 1 tsp roasted cumin powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves, chopped (for garnish)
  • Pomegranate seeds (optional, for garnish)

Instructions:

Prepare the Vadas:
  1. Grind the soaked urad dal into a smooth paste, adding minimal water.
  2. Add salt, asafoetida, and cumin seeds to the batter. Mix well until fluffy.
  3. Heat oil in a deep pan or wok.
  4. Drop spoonfuls of the batter into the hot oil and fry until golden brown.
  5. Remove the vadas and soak them in warm water for 10-15 minutes.
Assemble the Dahi Vadas:
  1. Gently squeeze out excess water from the soaked vadas and place them on a serving plate.
  2. Pour whisked yogurt over the vadas, ensuring they are fully covered.
  3. Drizzle tamarind chutney and green chutney on top.
  4. Sprinkle roasted cumin powder, red chili powder, and salt.
  5. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and pomegranate seeds, if using.
Serve:
  • Chill the dahi vadas for 30 minutes before serving for the best taste.
  • Enjoy this delicious and cooling snack with family and friends!
Enjoy your delightful Dahi Vada!
