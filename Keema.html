Keema Curry

A flavorful and spicy minced meat curry that pairs perfectly with naan or rice!

Ingredients:

  • 500g minced meat (keema)
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1 cup peas (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions:

Prepare the Base:
  1. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Add chopped onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies. Cook for 2 minutes until the raw smell disappears.
  3. Add tomato puree, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and coriander powder. Cook until the oil separates.
Cook the Keema:
  1. Add the minced meat to the pan and stir well to combine with the masala.
  2. Cook on medium heat for 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the meat is cooked through.
  3. If using peas, add them now and cook for another 5 minutes.
  4. Sprinkle garam masala and mix well. Adjust salt as needed.
Serve:
  • Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  • Serve hot with naan, roti, or steamed rice.
Enjoy your delicious Keema Curry!
