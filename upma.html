Upma Recipe

A simple and delicious South Indian breakfast dish!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup semolina (rava)
  • 2 tablespoons ghee or oil
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon urad dal
  • 1 teaspoon chana dal
  • 10-12 curry leaves
  • 2 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cups water
  • 1/4 cup chopped vegetables (carrots, peas, beans)
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice

Instructions:

Prepare the Semolina:
  1. Heat a pan and dry roast the semolina on low flame until it turns light golden and aromatic. Set aside.
Prepare the Tempering:
  1. Heat ghee or oil in the same pan.
  2. Add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
  3. Add urad dal, chana dal, curry leaves, and green chilies. Sauté for a few seconds.
  4. Add chopped onions and cook until they turn translucent.
  5. Add the chopped vegetables and sauté for 2-3 minutes.
Cook the Upma:
  1. Add 2 cups of water to the pan and bring it to a boil.
  2. Lower the flame and gradually add the roasted semolina while stirring continuously to avoid lumps.
  3. Cook on low flame, stirring occasionally, until the semolina absorbs all the water and the mixture thickens.
Finish and Serve:
  • Add salt, lemon juice, and mix well.
  • Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot.
Enjoy your flavorful and comforting Upma!
