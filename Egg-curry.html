Egg Curry

A rich and spicy egg curry that's perfect with rice or flatbreads!

Ingredients:

  • 4-6 boiled eggs
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2-3 green chilies, slit
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

Prepare the Eggs:
  1. Boil the eggs, peel them, and make small slits on the surface to allow the flavors to seep in.
  2. Heat 1 tbsp oil in a pan and lightly fry the eggs until golden. Set aside.
Cook the Curry:
  1. Heat the remaining oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  2. Add chopped onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  3. Stir in the ginger-garlic paste and green chilies. Cook for 2 minutes.
  4. Add the tomato puree, turmeric, red chili powder, coriander powder, and salt. Cook until the oil separates.
  5. Add 1-1.5 cups of water and bring the mixture to a boil.
  6. Lower the heat, add the fried eggs, and let them simmer for 5-7 minutes.
  7. Sprinkle garam masala and mix gently.
Serve:
  • Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  • Serve hot with steamed rice, naan, or roti.
Enjoy your delicious Egg Curry!
