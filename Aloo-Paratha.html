Aloo Paratha

A soft and flavorful Indian flatbread stuffed with spiced potato filling, perfect for breakfast or any meal!

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1/2 cup water (adjust as needed)
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3 medium potatoes (boiled and mashed)
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/4 cup chopped coriander leaves
  • 1 green chili (finely chopped)
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Butter or ghee for cooking

Instructions:

Prepare the Dough:
  1. In a mixing bowl, combine whole wheat flour, salt, and water to form a soft dough.
  2. Knead well and let it rest for 20 minutes, covered with a damp cloth.
Prepare the Filling:
  1. In a bowl, mix the boiled and mashed potatoes with cumin seeds, chopped coriander, green chili, red chili powder, garam masala, and salt.
  2. Ensure the mixture is smooth and lump-free.
Assemble the Paratha:
  1. Divide the dough and potato filling into equal portions.
  2. Roll out a dough ball into a small circle, place a portion of the filling in the center, and seal by bringing the edges together.
  3. Roll it gently into a larger circle, being careful not to let the filling spill out.
Cook the Paratha:
  1. Heat a tawa or skillet over medium heat.
  2. Place the rolled paratha on the hot tawa and cook for 1-2 minutes until bubbles appear.
  3. Flip and apply butter or ghee on both sides, cooking until golden brown.
Serve:
  • Serve hot with yogurt, pickle, or chutney of your choice.
Enjoy your delicious Aloo Paratha!
