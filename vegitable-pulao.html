Vegetable Pulao

A fragrant and wholesome rice dish loaded with vegetables and spices!

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, beans, and cauliflower)
  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 3-4 cloves
  • 2-3 cardamom pods
  • 1-inch cinnamon stick
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 2 tablespoons ghee or oil
  • 4 cups water
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions:

Prepare the Ingredients:
  1. Wash the basmati rice thoroughly and soak it in water for 30 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  2. Chop the mixed vegetables into small pieces.
Cook the Pulao:
  1. Heat ghee or oil in a large pan or pressure cooker.
  2. Add cumin seeds, cloves, cardamom, cinnamon, and bay leaves. Sauté until fragrant.
  3. Add the sliced onions and green chilies. Cook until the onions turn golden brown.
  4. Mix in the ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute.
  5. Add the chopped vegetables and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  6. Stir in the soaked and drained basmati rice. Mix gently to combine.
  7. Add 4 cups of water and salt to taste. Bring it to a boil.
  8. Cover with a lid and cook on low heat until the rice is cooked and the water is absorbed.
Serve:
  • Fluff the pulao gently with a fork.
  • Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  • Serve hot with raita or pickle.
Enjoy your flavorful Vegetable Pulao!
