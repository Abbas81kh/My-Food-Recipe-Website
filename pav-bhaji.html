Pav Bhaji

A popular Mumbai street food consisting of a spicy mashed vegetable curry served with buttery bread rolls.

Ingredients:

Bhaji:

  • 2 cups boiled and mashed vegetables (potatoes, peas, carrots, cauliflower)
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 cup finely chopped onions
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 cups chopped tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon pav bhaji masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons chopped coriander leaves

Pav:

  • 8 pav (bread rolls)
  • 2 tablespoons butter

Instructions:

Prepare the Bhaji:

  1. Heat butter in a large pan and add cumin seeds.
  2. Saut√© onions until golden brown, then add ginger-garlic paste.
  3. Add tomatoes, turmeric, and pav bhaji masala. Cook until the tomatoes are soft.
  4. Add the mashed vegetables, mix well, and cook for 5-7 minutes.
  5. Adjust salt and garnish with coriander leaves.

Prepare the Pav:

  1. Slice the pav horizontally and toast them on a pan with butter until golden brown.
Serve:
  • Serve the hot bhaji with buttered pav, chopped onions, lemon wedges, and extra butter on top.
Enjoy your tasty Pav Bhaji!
