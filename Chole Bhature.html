Chole Bhature

Chole Bhature is a popular North indian dish consisting od spicy chicken curry(chole) and deep-(fried) fluff bread (bhature). The chole is cooked with aromatic spices , onions and tomatos, while the bhature is made from a fermentef flour dough. its a hearty, flavored meal often Enjoyed with pickles and yogurt.

Ingredients:-

Ingredients For Chole:
  • 1 cup chickpeas (soaked overnight)
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2-3 green chilies, slit
  • 1 inch ginger, grated
  • 5-6 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala powder
  • 1 tsp amchur powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 cups water
  • Oil for frying
Ingredients For Bhature:
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp yogurt
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Water as needed
  • Oil for deep frying

Instructions For Chole

  1. Pressure Cook Chickpeas:Pressure cook the soaked chickpeas with 2 cups of water for 3-4 whistles. Let the pressure release naturally.
  2. Saute Spices:Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds, and let them splutter. Add chopped onions, green chilies, ginger, and garlic. Sauté until the onions turn golden brown.
  3. Add Spices and Tomato Paste:Add tomato paste, turmeric powder, red chili powder, coriander powder, garam masala powder, and amchur powder. Sauté for a few minutes until the raw smell disappears.
  4. Combine with Chickpeas:Add the pressure-cooked chickpeas and water to the masala. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer until the gravy thickens.
  5. Adjust Taste:Taste and adjust salt and spices as needed. Garnish with coriander leaves.
instructions For Bhature:
  1. Mix DoughIn a bowl, mix all-purpose flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, yogurt, and oil. Gradually add water to form a soft dough. Knead for 5-7 minutes.
  2. Rest the Dough:Cover the dough and let it rest for 30 minutes.
  3. Roll and Fry:Divide the dough into equal portions. Roll each portion into a ball and then roll it out into a flat, round shape. Deep fry the bhature in hot oil until golden brown and puffed up.
  4. serve:Serve hot Chole with fluffy Bhature.
Tips:
  • For a spicier Chole, add more red chili powder or green chilies
  • For a tangier flavor, add more amchur powder.
  • To make the Bhature extra fluffy, add a pinch of baking soda to the dough
  • Serve with a side of pickled onions or yogurt.
Enjoy your homemade Chole Bhature!!
