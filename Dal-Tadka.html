Dal Tadka

A comforting and flavorful Indian lentil dish, perfect for any meal!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup toor dal (split pigeon peas)
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 1 medium tomato, finely chopped
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • 2-3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1-inch ginger, grated
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 2 tbsp ghee or oil
  • 2 tbsp fresh coriander leaves, chopped
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

Cook the Dal:
  1. Wash the toor dal thoroughly and soak it for 30 minutes.
  2. In a pressure cooker, add the soaked dal, water, turmeric powder, and salt.
  3. Cook for 2-3 whistles until the dal is soft and mushy. Mash it lightly and set aside.
Prepare the Tadka (Tempering):
  1. Heat ghee or oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  3. Add garlic, ginger, and green chilies. Saut√© for a minute.
  4. Add chopped onions and cook until golden brown.
  5. Mix in the tomatoes and red chili powder. Cook until the tomatoes are soft.
Combine and Serve:
  1. Add the cooked dal to the tadka mixture and mix well.
  2. Simmer for 5 minutes, adjusting the consistency with water if needed.
  3. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
Serve:
  • Enjoy hot with steamed rice, roti, or naan.
Enjoy your comforting Dal Tadka!
