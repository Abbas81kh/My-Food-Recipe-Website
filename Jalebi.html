Jalebi

A crispy and sugary Indian dessert, perfect for any occasion!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 2 tbsp cornflour
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1/4 tsp yellow or orange food color
  • Oil or ghee for frying
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/4 tsp cardamom powder
  • A few saffron strands (optional)

Instructions:

Prepare the Batter:
  1. In a bowl, mix all-purpose flour, cornflour, and baking powder.
  2. Add yogurt and food color to the dry ingredients and whisk to form a smooth batter.
  3. Cover and let the batter ferment for 6-8 hours or overnight.
Make the Sugar Syrup:
  1. In a pan, combine sugar and water. Heat until the sugar dissolves completely.
  2. Add cardamom powder and saffron strands (if using) to the syrup. Boil until it reaches a one-string consistency.
  3. Keep the syrup warm.
Fry the Jalebi:
  1. Heat oil or ghee in a deep pan over medium heat.
  2. Fill the batter into a piping bag or squeeze bottle with a fine nozzle.
  3. Pipe the batter into the hot oil in spiral shapes.
  4. Fry until golden and crisp on both sides.
Soak and Serve:
  • Dip the fried jalebis into the warm sugar syrup for 1-2 minutes.
  • Remove and let the excess syrup drip off.
  • Serve hot or cold as per your preference.
Enjoy your homemade Jalebi!
