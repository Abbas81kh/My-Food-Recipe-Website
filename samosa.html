Samosa

A crispy and savory snack stuffed with a spiced potato filling, perfect for tea-time!

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup oil or ghee
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Water (as needed)
  • 3 medium potatoes (boiled and mashed)
  • 1/2 cup green peas (boiled)
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon amchur (dry mango powder)
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for frying

Instructions:

Prepare the Dough:
  1. In a bowl, mix all-purpose flour, salt, and oil or ghee until the mixture is crumbly.
  2. Add water gradually and knead into a firm dough.
  3. Cover the dough and let it rest for 30 minutes.
Prepare the Filling:
  1. Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds.
  2. Once they splutter, add mashed potatoes, green peas, turmeric, red chili powder, garam masala, amchur, and salt.
  3. Mix well and cook for 2-3 minutes. Let the filling cool.
Assemble the Samosa:
  1. Divide the dough into equal portions and roll out into small circles.
  2. Cut each circle in half to form two semicircles.
  3. Shape the semicircle into a cone and seal the edges with water.
  4. Fill the cone with the prepared potato mixture and seal the top by pinching the edges.
Fry the Samosas:
  1. Heat oil in a deep pan over medium heat.
  2. Fry the samosas in batches until they are golden brown and crispy.
  3. Remove and drain on paper towels.
Serve:
  • Serve hot with tamarind chutney, mint chutney, or ketchup.
Enjoy your crispy and delicious Samosas!
